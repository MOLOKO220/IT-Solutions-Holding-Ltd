Art and Media

Accounting

The arts and media sectors are one of the most diverse, dynamic and important sectors in the UK economy. This is also one of the most difficult and unpredictable moments.

Entertainment and media companies, as a key part of the economy and wider cultural life, have long attracted investments both domestically and abroad. Our art institutions have a worldwide reputation with artists, musicians and performers who are the lifeblood of the industry. But in the creative industries, nothing stands still.
We attend many major international art fairs, events and exhibitions, and host a number of industry events. As many of them go online and study how they will act in the future, we follow the discussions through our many clients and acquaintances. Only when we are connected, informed and involved can we advise our clients and think creatively to help them achieve their business goals.
We represent major UK art galleries, artists, actors, musicians, theaters, producers and production companies, providing strategic advice on business structuring and tax planning, as well as day-to-day support in accounting, VAT and tax compliance.

Technological

accounting

Today's tech companies are fast growing, agile and flexible. Therefore, they need appropriate support.

In the tech sector, new products, fast-growing markets, and intense competition mean that companies must constantly come up with big ideas, lean processes, and smart solutions to keep up.
Our dedicated technical accounting and consulting team takes the same approach. Working with tech companies of all shapes and sizes for years, we understand the challenges our customers face and show the same tenacity, adaptability and ability to find opportunities in our work.
We can help you with funding, advise on how to protect and use your intellectual property, help you develop tax-efficient ways to retain your best talent, and more. Our ultimate goal is to ensure you get the most out of the creativity that lies at the heart of your business.
And when it comes to growing and developing of your IT business, our dedicated team can support you to make the most of any tax breaks you may qualify for, including business incentives.

Retail and wholesale

accounting

The retail industry is undergoing seismic change. But in the midst of all this mess, there is one thing you can rely on: our support.

Our team of retail and wholesalers is dedicated to helping you navigate rapidly evolving technology, consumer behavior and retail pressures. We provide accounting and tax advice to a wide range of clients from a wide variety of categories such as health and beauty, fashion, furniture, gifts and food.
Whether you are a retail startup looking to help improve their business model, a small to medium-sized enterprise looking to open new outlets, or a mature wholesaler trying to adapt, we offer personalized advice and hands-on support to help you. take the next step in your development.
Our experienced accountants can also help you use your business and financial records to track your management data, provide cash flow forecasting, and develop many other back-office solutions to help your business move forward. And since all companies are individual, we tailor our services and our costs so that you get exactly what you need, without the waste and fuss.
