Audit and assurance

Reliable financial information crucial to your business: exceptional quality, time savings and deeper understanding.
Our team of specialists does not consider the audit and assurance responsibilities a tedious undertaking. We see them as an important opportunity to help your organization improve and grow. Whatever we do for you - be it external, internal audit or any of our full range of assurance services - our ultimate goal is to help you achieve your broader business goals.
We provide audit services to thousands of clients across the UK, from sophisticated corporate businesses to small and medium-sized enterprises, across a wide range of industry sectors.

01. External audit

Our robust external audits do more than mark all compliance fields - they enable you to make confident and informed decisions based on a clear and concise independent assessment of your organization's finances. Using a risk-based methodology, we take the time it takes to get to know your business from the inside out and focus our services on the key areas of your financial data. There is no wasted time or effort and the final analysis is targeted and personalized.

Our external audit services include:

  • +
    Mandatory audit
  • +
    Optional audits
  • +
    Other Limited and Specific Assurance Engagements
  • +
    Both UK GAAP and IFRS

02. Internal audit

Our audit and assurance team offers a fresh approach to internal audit. Our risk-based approach places audit in the context of your own risk management system, making it a cornerstone of corporate governance. We work with your Board of Directors and Audit Committee to gain a deeper understanding of the financial, operational and corporate risks of your business, as well as deliver a clear, tailored and practical report, free of lengthy disclaimers and disclaimers.

Our internal audit process includes:

  • +
    Collaborative strategic planning: identifying risks and how they relate to your strategic goals
  • +
    Customized assignment plans: detailed scope and objectives along with associated risks
  • +
    Clear reporting: summary, conclusions and action plan for each issue
  • +
    Proactive Observation: Quarterly audits to track progress on action plans
  • +
    Forward-looking annual report: auditor's report on general internal control to inform the board of directors and comment on the risk registers
